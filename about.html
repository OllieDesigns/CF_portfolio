About Ollie

Hey there! My name is Ollie and I am a pastry chef and UX designer currently living in Minnesota.

I have a background in fine pastry and the culinary arts paired with an education from the Culinary Institute of America. As I’ve been working and learning as a pastry chef, I’ve realized how important it is to make products that are simple, enjoyable, and accessible which has added perspective as a UX designer. With five years in the service and food industry I’m able to multitask, work under pressure, and switch skills at a moment's notice. Being able to focus in a fast paced environment has created a strong basis in on the go problem solving and strong communicative skills, whether I’m presenting an issue, or solving one. I love spending my free time playing video games, creating art, and finding new restaurants to eat at or new grocery stores to wander around in. I’m a big fan of exploring different places and topics, which led me to take CareerFoundry’s UX design course, giving me new skills in research wireframing and prototyping, as well as a slew of other skills!
