About Ollie

Hey there! My name is Ollie and I am a pastry chef and UX designer currently living in Minnesota.

I have a background in fine pastry and the culinary arts paired with an education from theCulinary Institute of America. As I’ve been working and learning as a pastry chef, I’verealized how important it is to make products that are simple, enjoyable, and accessiblewhich has added perspective as a UX designer.With five years in the service and food industry I’m able to multitask, work under pressure,and switch skills at a moment's notice. Being able to focus in a fast paced environment hascreated a strong basis in on the go problem solving and strong communicative skills, whetherI’m presenting an issue, or solving one.I love spending my free time playing video games, creating art, and finding new restaurants to eatat or new grocery stores to wander around in. I’m a big fan of exploring different places and topics,which led me to take CareerFoundry’s UX design course, giving me new skills in research wireframingand prototyping, as well as a slew of other skills!
